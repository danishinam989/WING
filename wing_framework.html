Support Level Framework
Wing's Stepped Care model delivers the right level of support to the right person at the right time - without clinical overreach.
Wing uses a four-level Stepped Care framework - the gold standard model recommended by WHO, NICE and the HSE for mental health triage. Assessment scores from DASS-21, Rosenberg and SPM combine to determine the appropriate level. The user receives a plain-language recommendation and specific next steps. No jargon. No clinical diagnosis. Just clarity.
